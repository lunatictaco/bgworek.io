biography

I'm an experimental and computational biophysicist. My interests lie in connecting experimental measurements to theoretical results. I'm motivated to pursue the multidisiplinary field of biophysics, integrating my knowledge on molecular biology, biochemistry, physics, and computer science. My mission is to solve complex biological problems that relate to neurological diseases, such as, Alzheimer's disease and Schizophrenia. My expertise is in the field of cell signaling, programmed cell death, optogenetics, and molecular dynamics. I also like to yap and do weird stuff.
